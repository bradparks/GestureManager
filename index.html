Gesture Manager

Gesture manager is a general purpose gesture recognizer, written in the most extensible way. Offers out of the box recognition for multi touch gestures like:


Tap. Unlimited amount of touch points.

LongTap. Unlimited amount of touch points.

Swipe. Unlimited amount of touch points.

Double Tap. One touch point.

Pinch/Zoom/Move. Two touch point.

Pinch 5. Five fingers pinch (iOS close app gesture).
